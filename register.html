Register for Hackegerton

Join us for an exciting hackathon experience! Build innovative projects, network with peers, and compete for prizes. Registration opens January 12.

How to Register

Registering for Hackegerton is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps to secure your spot in the hackathon.

1

Fill Out Registration Form

Complete the registration form with your personal details, university information, and contact details. Make sure all information is accurate.

2

Choose Your Track

Select your preferred track: AI & Data, Web / Mobile, FinTech / Blockchain, or Sustainability / HealthTech. You can also indicate if you're open to any track.

3

Team Information

Indicate if you're registering as an individual or as part of a team. If you have team members, provide their details. You can also find teammates through our platform.

4

Select Event Type

Choose between the main hackathon (299 KSH registration fee) or the free mini hackathon on February 6th (limited to 50 participants, first come first served).

5

Complete Payment

If registering for the main hackathon, complete the payment of 299 KSH through our secure payment gateway. Payment confirmation will be sent via email.

6

Confirmation

Once registered, you'll receive a confirmation email with all the details, schedule, and next steps. Join our communication channels for updates and networking.

Registration Fees & Options

Choose the registration option that works best for you. We offer flexible options to accommodate all participants.

Main Hackathon

Full access to the complete hackathon experience from January 12 to February 28. Includes all workshops, mentorship, and the grand finale event. Registration fee: 299 KSH.

Mini Hackathon

Free access to the mini in-person hackathon on February 6th. Limited to 50 participants only - register early to secure your spot! Perfect for trying out the hackathon experience.

Payment Methods

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and other secure payment methods. Payment confirmation will be sent immediately after successful transaction.

What's Included

Access to workshops, mentorship sessions, networking events, project presentations, judging, awards ceremony, and prizes. Plus meals and refreshments during in-person events.

Registration Benefits

  • Full hackathon access: Participate in all events from start to finish
  • Workshops & mentorship: Learn from industry experts and get guidance
  • Networking opportunities: Connect with peers, mentors, and potential collaborators
  • Project showcase: Present your project to judges and the community
  • Prizes & recognition: Compete for exciting prizes and get recognition for your innovation

After Registration

Once you've registered, here's what happens next and how to make the most of your hackathon experience.

Confirmation Email

You'll receive a confirmation email with your registration details, schedule, and important information. Keep this email for reference.

Join Communication Channels

Access our Discord, Slack, or other communication platforms to connect with other participants, ask questions, and stay updated.

Team Formation

If you're looking for teammates, use our team formation channels. You can also register as a complete team if you already have members.

Pre-Hackathon Preparation

Attend pre-hackathon workshops and webinars. Review resources, set up your development environment, and brainstorm project ideas.

Building Phase

Start building your project from January 12. Use our resources, attend mentorship sessions, and collaborate with your team.

Presentation & Judging

Prepare your project presentation for February 27. Present to judges and showcase your innovation. Winners will be announced on February 28.

Tips for a Successful Hackathon

  • Start preparing early - set up your development environment before the hackathon begins
  • Form a diverse team with complementary skills
  • Attend all workshops and mentorship sessions
  • Network actively with other participants and mentors
  • Focus on solving a real problem with your project
  • Practice your presentation and demo before the final day
  • Have fun and enjoy the learning experience!
